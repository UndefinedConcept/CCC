Campus Coders Crew

Empowering students to collaborate, innovate, and grow. Join our community of developers and build websites for various campus groups.

Who are we?

We are a group of students interested in hands on experience in software engineering. Our club takes students out of the typical classroom setting and into a community of thinkers, builders, and dreamers.

Apply For a Website

Eligibility

All UMD students and clubs can request a website! Not from UMD? Feel free to email us for follow up details.

Process

All requests will be fulfilled! If no teams are currently available, you’ll be placed on a waitlist.

Timeline

Placement depends on team availability and demand. All respondents will be kept up to date with the status of their application.

Cost

Our base plan is free. For back end and server-side applications, pricing will adjust accordingly.
